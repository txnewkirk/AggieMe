FAQ

What is AggieME?

  • AggieMe is an exclusive social media platform for NCAT students to network with each other and engage within the aggie community. Students can follow, message, and share content with each other just like any other social media platform.

How do I know which groups to join?

  • AggieMe will prompt you with a survey asking you what are 3 of your top traits. Then it will give you recommended communities to join.

What is a Community Feed? How can I set up my Community Feed?

  • Your new Page comes with a Feed that is separate from your personal profile’s Feed and is dedicated to your Page presence. Anyone with AggieME access to the new Page can access and view this Feed. If you have AggieMe access, you can see partners and other pages to curate the content that appears in the Feed.

How can I access my profile?



  • You can access your profile after logging in and clicking “profile” in the menu bar at the top of the screen.

Can I retake the survey?



  • The survey can only be taken once for new users.

Can I view new recommendations after signing up ?

  • You cannot view new recommendations, but you may explore more communities to join.

What if I don’t see a community I’m interested in?

  • You may create your own community by selecting  the “Create Community” button in your user profile.
